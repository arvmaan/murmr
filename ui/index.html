Welcome to murmr 👋

To dictate anywhere, murmr needs three macOS permissions. Grant them, then relaunch murmr:

  1. Input Monitoring — detect your hotkey
  2. Accessibility — paste at your cursor
  3. Microphone — record (prompted on first use)

Then hold ⌘⇧K, speak, and release.
